TNAU RABI CROP GUIDE — TAMIL NADU

WHEAT: pH 6.0-7.5. Temp 10-25C. Sow November-December. Duration 110-120 days. Irrigate at CRI, tillering, jointing, flowering, grain filling stages.

CHICKPEA: pH 6.0-7.5. Well-drained soil. Duration 95-110 days. Sow October-November. Inoculate with Mesorhizobium. Sensitive to waterlogging. Critical irrigation at pod initiation.

SUNFLOWER: pH 6.5-8.0. Duration 90-100 days. Sow October-November. Boron deficiency causes empty heads — apply borax 1 kg/ha at bud stage. Bee pollination improves yield 20-30%.

CORIANDER: pH 6.5-7.5. Duration 90-100 days. Sow October-November. Fine tilth required. Susceptible to stem rot in wet conditions.

MUSTARD: pH 6.5-7.5. Duration 90-110 days. Sulfur 20 kg/ha critical for oil content. Control aphids at flowering.

SAFFLOWER: pH 6.5-8.0. Duration 130-150 days. Highly drought tolerant. Good choice for dry regions with poor soil.

POTATO (Nilgiris): pH 5.5-6.5. Duration 75-90 days. Seed tubers 50-60g treated with Mancozeb. Earth up twice at 25 and 45 DAS. Spray for late blight preventively.

INTERCROPPING: Chickpea+Mustard 6:2 row ratio. Wheat+Chickpea reduces aphids.
