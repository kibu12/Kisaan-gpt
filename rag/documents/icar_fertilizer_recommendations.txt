ICAR FERTILIZER RECOMMENDATIONS

RICE: N 120, P 60, K 60 kg/ha. Split N: 50% basal, 25% tillering, 25% panicle. Use DAP for P.
WHEAT: N 120, P 60, K 40 kg/ha. Split N: 50% basal, 25% CRI, 25% flag leaf.
MAIZE: N 150, P 75, K 60 kg/ha. Split N in 3: basal, knee-high, tasseling.
GROUNDNUT: N 25, P 50, K 75 kg/ha. Inoculate with Rhizobium. Gypsum 500 kg/ha at pegging.
COTTON: N 120, P 60, K 60 kg/ha. Split N in 4. Apply borax 1 kg/ha at bud stage.
CHICKPEA: N 20, P 40, K 20 kg/ha. Inoculate with Mesorhizobium.
SUGARCANE: N 275, P 62, K 112 kg/ha per year.
SUNFLOWER: N 90, P 60, K 60 kg/ha. Boron foliar 0.2% at bud stage.

RULES:
- Always apply P and K as basal dose before sowing.
- Split nitrogen to reduce leaching. Never apply all N as basal.
- Neem-coated urea improves nitrogen efficiency by 10-15%.
- Combine organic and inorganic fertilizers for best results.
- 4R: Right source, Right rate, Right time, Right place.
