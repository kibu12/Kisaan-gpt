TNAU KHARIF CROP GUIDE — TAMIL NADU

RICE: pH 5.5-7.0. Temp 20-35C. Humidity above 80%. Rainfall 150-200mm. Duration 105-115 days. Sow June-July. Maintain 2-5cm standing water. Zinc deficiency common — apply ZnSO4 25 kg/ha.

GROUNDNUT: pH 6.0-7.0. Sandy loam. Rainfall 500-600mm. Duration 100-115 days. Seed treat with Rhizobium. Gypsum at pegging stage mandatory for pod development.

MAIZE: pH 6.0-7.5. Rainfall 600-900mm. Duration 90-100 days. Spacing 60x25cm. Earthing up at 30 DAS.

COTTON: pH 6.5-8.0. Black cotton soil preferred. Rainfall 600-800mm. Duration 180-200 days. Spacing 90x45cm. Watch for bollworm.

SORGHUM: pH 6.5-8.0. Rainfall 400-600mm. Duration 100-110 days. Drought tolerant — good for low rainfall areas.

BAJRA: pH 6.0-7.5. Sandy loam. Rainfall 400-600mm. Duration 75-90 days. Tolerates poor soils. Good when N and K are low.

BLACKGRAM: pH 6.5-7.5. Duration 60-70 days. Short duration legume — restores soil N in rotation.

CROP ROTATION: Rice-Groundnut-Blackgram. Cotton-Maize-Chickpea.
GREEN MANURE: Dhaincha adds 80-100 kg N/ha. Plough in at 45 days.
