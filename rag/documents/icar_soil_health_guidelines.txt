ICAR SOIL HEALTH GUIDELINES

SOIL TESTING: Irrigated land every 2 years. Rainfed every 3 years.
Collect 8-10 sub-samples per field. Sample depth 0-15 cm. Take 500g composite sample.

NUTRIENT MANAGEMENT:
- Apply FYM 10 tonnes/ha as base for all crops.
- FYM replaces 25% of recommended chemical N.
- Biofertilizers: Rhizobium for legumes, Azospirillum for cereals.

MICRONUTRIENTS:
- Zinc deficiency: Apply ZnSO4 25 kg/ha or foliar 0.5%.
- Iron deficiency: FeSO4 foliar 0.5% at 30 and 45 DAS.
- Boron deficiency: Borax 1 kg/ha soil or 0.2% foliar.

ORGANIC CARBON:
- Never burn crop residues. Incorporate all organic matter.
- 1% increase in OC retains 83,000 litres water per hectare.

SOIL ACIDITY:
- Apply lime 4-6 weeks before sowing.
- Per 0.5 pH unit rise needed: apply 1 tonne CaCO3/ha.
- Dolomite preferred when Mg is also deficient.

SALINITY:
- Apply gypsum 5-10 tonnes/ha for sodic soils.
- Leach with excess irrigation before first crop.
