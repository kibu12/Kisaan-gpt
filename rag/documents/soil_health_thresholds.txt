SOIL HEALTH CRITICAL THRESHOLDS — ICAR

pH:
Below 5.5: CRITICAL — Aluminium toxicity. Apply lime 2-3 tonnes/ha immediately.
5.5 to 6.0: WARNING — Apply lime 1 tonne/ha this season.
6.0 to 7.5: OPTIMAL — Most crops perform well.
7.5 to 8.5: ALKALINE — Apply gypsum 2 tonnes/ha.
Above 8.5: CRITICAL — Soil reclamation required before planting.

Organic Carbon (OC):
Below 0.5%: CRITICAL — Apply FYM 10 tonnes/ha every season.
0.5 to 0.75%: LOW — Apply FYM 5 tonnes/ha.
Above 0.75%: ADEQUATE.

EC (Electrical Conductivity):
Above 4 dS/m: CRITICAL — Salt damage. Leach and apply gypsum.
2 to 4 dS/m: WARNING — Use salt-tolerant varieties only.

Nitrogen (N) kg/ha:
Below 280: LOW. 280-560: MEDIUM. Above 560: HIGH.

Phosphorus (P) kg/ha:
Below 10: LOW. 10-25: MEDIUM. Above 25: HIGH.

Potassium (K) kg/ha:
Below 108: LOW. 108-280: MEDIUM. Above 280: HIGH.

NOTE: pH 5.5 is a cliff not a slope. Below this point aluminium becomes toxic to crop roots.
